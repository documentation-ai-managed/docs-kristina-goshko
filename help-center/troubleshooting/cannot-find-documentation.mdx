---
title: Cannot Find Documentation
description: Quick steps if documentation pages are missing.
---

## Symptoms

- Documentation page not found
- Page shows "404" or missing error

## Causes

- Page was deleted or moved
- Incorrect URL entered

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for your page">
    Use the search bar at the top of Kristina Goshko's docs to look for the page name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact admin">
    If missing, email support@kristinagoshko.com to restore the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
