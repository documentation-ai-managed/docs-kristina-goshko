## Overview

Group and manage related documentation efficiently for your projects.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project folder">
    Click "New Project" in the sidebar and give your folder a clear name (e.g., Project Apollo).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add pages and sections">
    Inside your project, click "Add Page" to create documentation for features, teams, or guides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange or reorder">
    Drag and drop pages to reorder or group them by topic for easier access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
