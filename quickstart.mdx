## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub account for version control integration

<Callout kind="info">

Prepare your project details, such as the space name and initial documents. This setup takes under 5 minutes.

</Callout>

## Account Setup and Space Creation

Follow these steps to create your Kristina Goshko documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Visit `https://kristinagoshko.com` and click **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your email, create a password, and verify your account via email.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="plus">

    After login, select **New Space** from the dashboard.

    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, choose a brand color such as `#3B82F6`, and select **Create**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Document" icon="file-text">

    In your new space, click **New Document**.

    Name it `introduction.mdx` and start editing with the built-in MDX editor.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
      ````mdx
      ---
      title: Introduction
      description: Welcome to my docs.
      ---

      ## Overview

      This is your first document.
      ````

      ```markdown
      # Introduction

      Welcome to my documentation space.
      ```
    </CodeGroup>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and Share" icon="globe">

    Click **Publish** to make your space live.

    Copy the shareable URL, such as `https://kristinagoshko.com/spaces/my-project-docs`.

  </Step>
</Steps>
